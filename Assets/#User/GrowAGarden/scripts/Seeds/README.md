# Seed Definitions

## One-Time Harvest
Plant is pulled on harvest — seed must be replanted.

| Crop    | Buy | Sell | Grow Time | Notes               |
|---------|-----|------|-----------|---------------------|
| Carrot  | 10  | 15   | 1 min     | Baseline budget option |
| Turnip  | 18  | 28   | 2 min     | Step up, better margins |
| Pumpkin | 60  | 110  | 12 min    | Premium, slow, big payout |

## Regrowable *(not yet implemented)*
Fruit is harvested — plant stem remains and regrows fruit on a repeating timer.

| Crop     | Seed Buy | Per Harvest Sell | Stem Grow Time | Fruit Grow Time | Notes                          |
|----------|----------|-----------------|----------------|-----------------|--------------------------------|
| Tomato   | 30       | 18              | 4 min          | 2 min           | Breaks even ~2 harvests        |
| Corn     | 35       | 22              | 5 min          | 3 min           | Slower, better per harvest     |
| Eggplant | 45       | 25              | 5 min          | 3 min           | Mid-tier regrowable            |
